Lasagna

Description

A cheesy lasagna recipe

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs ground beef
  • .25 cups water
  • 1 oz taco seasoning
  • 12 tortillas
  • 14 ounces refired beans
  • 3 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 8 ounces taco sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. Cook beef, add water and taco seasoning
  3. Put everything in tortillas in a casserole dish
  4. Bake 20-25 minutes
